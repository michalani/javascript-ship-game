Help Info

To setup the game click any cell and press:
U for submarine= (max 1)
5,6,7,8 or 9 for fuel= (Unlimited)
O for obstacle = (Unlimited)
K for killer= (Unlimited)

Warning this game won't work without a submarine entity!

After setting up the board press to play
use W,A,X,D to move the submarine

To win collect radioactive nuclear fuel and
do not let the killer(s) submarine get close to you
Have fun & good luck :)
